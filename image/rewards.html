Back to School Rewards!

We’re making back to school shopping fun for everyone this year with Back to School Rewards. Shop at Mall of America and redeem your receipts for passes to your favorite MOA attractions including Nickelodeon Universe, Moose Mountain Adventure Golf and More! The process is simple, spend $150 at MOA from August 1 – September 6, 2021, and redeem for rewards on us!  

All MOA purchases must be made between August 1 – September 6, 2021, to qualify. Receipts must be uploaded to the Mall of America App.  

Redemption Periods:
Nickelodeon Universe Wristband rewards may be redeemed August 23, 2021 – October 17, 2021. All other rewards may be redeemed starting August 1, 2021 – October 17, 2021.

Back To School Rewards Terms + Conditions

Promotion Dates for Qualifying Mall of America® Purchases August 1, 2021 – September 6, 2021

Redemption / Upload Periods:
Nickelodeon Universe® may be redeemed from August 23, 2021 – October 17, 2021. (App upload only)
All other rewards may be redeemed from August 1, 2021 – October 17, 2021. (App upload only)

Blackout Dates
None

Rewards

  • Nickelodeon Universe – One Unlimited Ride Wristband (Unlimited Rewards) 
  • Moose Mountain – Two Passes (1,500 Rewards)
  • Rock of Ages – Two Passes (1,500 Rewards) 
  • Crayola Experience – One General Admission Ticket (1,500 Rewards) 
  • Fair on Four – One Free Blue Horse Pizza (1,500 Rewards) 
  • SEA LIFE Minnesota Aquarium – One General Admission Ticket (1,000 Rewards) 
  • Sugar Factory – Free Milkshake (Dine-in Only 1,500 Rewards)
  • Donate your Nickelodeon Universe Wristband Rewards to Way to Grow, a local MN Charity Partner. 

Rules
You must be at least 18 years of age to participate in promotion.
Limit 6 rewards per person during promotion.
Limit 5 receipts per store, per person, per day.

Some rewards have limited quantities available.
Rewards will be distributed for each $150 spent at MOA®.
All MOA purchases must be made between August 1, 2021 – September 6, 2021
to qualify.
Only original store printed receipts accepted. No photocopies.
Emailed receipts must be submitted via the Mall of America or Ziploop App.

Purchases made on a retailer website or online store do not qualify.
Must present photo ID at guest services to receive promotional Nickelodeon Universe Any Day Unlimited Ride Wristband.
Wristbands are not for resale and have no cash value.

RECEIPTS MUST BE FROM PERSONAL PURCHASES ONLY AT MALL OF AMERICA. MALL OF AMERICA RESERVES THE RIGHT TO REFUSE ANY SUSPICIOUS RECEIPTS.
